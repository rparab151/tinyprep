## Full Description

TinyPrep is a free meal-prep planner for busy toddler parents. Build a weekly plan, organize prep-day batch cooking, save favorite meals, track foods as accepted, trying, or retry later, and generate a deduplicated grocery list.

TinyPrep is designed to work without account signup and stores planning preferences locally on your device by default.

Safety note: TinyPrep is for general meal-prep organization only. It is not medical advice. Always check allergies, follow pediatrician guidance, use age-appropriate textures and choking-safety practices, and reheat and store food safely.
