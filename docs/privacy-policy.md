# TinyPrep Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: Replace before submission.

TinyPrep is designed as a free, offline-first daily toddler meal app.

## Data Collection

TinyPrep does not require account signup and does not collect child names, birthdates, health data, sensitive personal information, advertising identifiers, or analytics events.

## Local Storage

Meal preferences, favorites, food status labels, and grocery checklist state are stored locally on the user's device.

## Ads, Subscriptions, and Analytics

TinyPrep does not include ads, subscriptions, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Data Sharing

TinyPrep does not sell personal data. The production app should keep this no-sale posture unless the privacy design is reviewed and this policy is updated.

## Safety Disclaimer

TinyPrep is not medical advice. Parents and caregivers should check allergies, follow pediatrician guidance, use age-appropriate texture and choking safety practices, and reheat and store food safely.
